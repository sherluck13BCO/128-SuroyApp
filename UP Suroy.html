Welcome to

University of the Philippines Cebu

UP Cebu envisions itself to be a world-class university known for academic excellence. It will be a regional center for cultural, social, and human development.

It will serve the Filipino people, especially those in Central Visayas, by promoting excellent undergraduate and gradute courses, and short-term trainings, which will be anchored on liberal education, human rights, equality, and people empowerment, aimed at addressing the quality of life of its students and other stakeholders.
